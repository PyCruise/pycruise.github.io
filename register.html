Register

The registration process for PyCruise is going to work differently than it does at most conferences. Read this page carefully!

If you want to register, please fill out the form below. Within 24 hours, you'll get an email with our group code. Once you have that, you'll need to call Carnival and reserve your room. You can either pay the full amount, or you can put down $200 per person.

When you call, make sure to give them our group code! Without it, you're not actually attending the conference.

If you're unable to call Carnival for whatever reason, let me know, and we'll work something out. Once the initial reservation is made, all other transactions can be done online.

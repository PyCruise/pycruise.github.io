Help out!

Want to help PyCruise be the best gathering of minds that it possibly can be? Why not sign up sponsor or be a volunteer?

Sponsor

PyCruise does not charge for tickets, so we're looking for sponsors that will allow us to:

  • Offset the cost of t-shirts
  • Fill the financial aid coffers
  • Have private PyCruise events, both on the boat and on shore
  • Print schedules and badges
  • Pay for Internet access, walkie-talkies, and anything else to help facilitate communication amongst staff

If you are interested in sponsoring us, please contact the PyCruise chair via the contact form.

Volunteer

PyCruise is going to need help, both at sea and on shore. Currently, we're looking for people who want to help out in the following departments:

  • Website design and maintenance
  • Shirt and schedule designers
  • Staff members for on-ship help
  • Open Space organizers
  • Diversity chair
  • Financial aid liaison

If you can help out, please, use the contact form and let us know!
